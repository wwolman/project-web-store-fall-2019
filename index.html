Arbor A-Frame

A new twist on an old favorite, the A-Frame is known for its relentless edge hold and unparalleled stability at speed and is now built with a directional, System Camber design. The newly redesigned chassis along with the addition of Grip-Tech contact points and Uprise fenders create a carving machine with an elevated ability to rail on edge and charge through chop. Arbor Annex

The Annex is the newest and most technical creation to stem from the ongoing collaboration between Bryan Iguchi and Arbor Snowboards. As Bryan's riding progressed to a state where he spends most of his time riding big-mountain lines, he needed a directional evolution of the fundamental performance we built into the Iguchi Pro Camber. Built on our System Camber technology, the Annex features a longer, drawn out nose with additional taper and a stub tail. The combination of these three features allow the board to float through powder effortlessly with increased power and drive when setting an edge. The Bio-Plastic Topsheet binds a Powerply stringer with our brand-new Carbon Vector Net reinforcements resulting in evenly distributed torsional power and accelerated response from tip to tail. The Annex features our extra-lightweight FSC Certified Highland ll Core, Recycled Steel Edges, and Entropy Bio Resin, making it both the principal of performance and sustainability in the Arbor line. The base graphic "scratches the surface of space an time" featuring artwork from Bryan's Art of Erosion gallery work.

Arbor Cask

The Cask is a high volume, short length, System Camber snowboard designed for resort powder days that require a board with the versatility to handle the gamut of conditions you'll experience from your first untouched run to your last rally through the chop. A long drawn out nose for effortless float is paired with camber under foot and a short stub tail to maximize control through deep snow. Pow-Tech contact points under the back foot create a stronger edge to snow connection while Uprise Fenders soften edge-to-edge transition. The Cask is so much fun, you won't want to head home until you've ripped every last secret stash on the mountain.

Arbor Shiloh

The Shiloh is a versatile mountain twin tailored for experienced freeriders who need a board to deliver quick edge-to-edge performance, snappy takeoffs and stable landings.
